Message From Principal
Dear Students and Parents, As Nelson Mandela once said: “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.'
We at Sharm El Sheikh College find it our duty to nurture today's children as they are tomorrow's future. We instil in our children the love of seeking knowledge and the belief that it is an ongoing, never-ending process. We them for the ever-changing world around us, encouraging them to become design thinkers and prepare entrepreneurs within the expanding global community of the 21st Century. We can achieve this by instilling SSC's Personal Core Values within our daily school life, assisting them to face the unknown and be ready for any unexpected circumstance. We also believe that fulfilling these goals should be a joint venture shared by both the School and the Parents.
Together, we can achieve the impossible and make it a reality.
Together we can set the roots of knowledgeable, creative, intellectual and passionate individuals, who will have positive and lasting impacts on their society and future world.
Let's all follow the advice of Les Brown: “Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you’ll land among the stars." School Principal

Doris Hanna, MAED
Mission

SSC is dedicated to providing students with Project Based Learning environment to deepen their knowledge, skills and understanding to achieve success in college, career, and life.

Vision

The vision of Sharm el Sheikh College is to provide a stimulating environment in which each child can develop fully as an individual through utilizing Project Based Learning methods, to meet the challenges of the 21st Century.

Campus Facilities
Library:

This room is dedicated for academic resource and enrichment  programmes. It includes a huge amount of fiction and non-fiction books. Furthermore, the library fosters a teacher  training center.
Art workshop:

With the introduction of an Art Specialist to the Fine Arts programme, we have dedicated a room where students will create, learn and showcase different aspects of fine arts.
Music Room:

The music room is the central venue to host the SSC music programme and the school orchestra.
Play Room:

The KG department contains a Free Choice Area that is equipped with all the necessary toys and educational games that enhances the students’ social and emotional development.
Science Lab:

The School campus has a science lab that is outfitted with gas and water pipes. In addition, it is equipped with benches and a Science prep room.
Rational Area:

The School campus has a science lab that is outfitted with gas and water pipes. In addition, it is equipped with benches and a Science prep room.
Atheletics:

Athletics consists of the PE programme at SSC, as well as junior teams. We are committed to offering a broad range of sports and activities for the students to participate in. SSC is involved in most of the major sports that you would find in any reputable school; dodge ball, football, volleyball, basketball and table tennis, are our main sports. We compete in the schools’ local league and we look forward to compete in the Cairo League against other schools.
