Sharm El-sheikh college

Sharm El Sheikh College aims is to provide opportunities for a better future through providing students with the 21st Century skills and quality education that meets the needs of the community. Children come to an understanding that they live in a special unique place,and that they need to have tolerance for all. Sharm el Sheikh College works in partnership with parents applying the innovated and enriched Egyptian National Curriculum.
